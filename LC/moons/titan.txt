 8-Titan
 
 ----------------------
 
 CONDITIONS: A frozen, flat landscape.
 
 HISTORY: It looks like this moon was mined for resources. It's 
 easy to get lost within the giant industrial complex. There are 
 many entrances to it littered about the landscape.
 
 FAUNA: Dangerous entities have been rumored to take residence in 
 the vast network of tunnels.
